<p><ul><li>Mechanical Suspension Seats</li><li>Air Suspension Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forklift seats market is primarily divided into mechanical suspension and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks, providing a stable ride, suitable for general applications. In contrast, air suspension seats utilize air-filled bladders that adjust to the operator's weight and movements, offering superior comfort and reduced fatigue during prolonged use. These seats enhance productivity by improving operator comfort, making them essential for heavy-duty environments where vibration and bumps are prevalent.</p></p>
