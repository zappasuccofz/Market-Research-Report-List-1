<p><p>The Travel Reducer market is categorized primarily into flange-mounted and shaft-mounted types, both serving essential functions in motion control systems. Flange-mounted travel reducers are designed for direct attachment to other equipment, offering stability and ease of installation. In contrast, shaft-mounted reducers connect directly to the shafts of motors or other rotating devices, allowing for more compact configurations. Both types enhance operational efficiency by providing gearing solutions that reduce speed while increasing torque in various industrial applications.</p></p>
