<p><p>Investor ESG software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software allows users to access ESG tools and data online, offering scalability, automatic updates, and remote accessibility, which is ideal for investors seeking flexibility. Conversely, on-premise software is installed locally on an organization's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring a higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to differing operational needs and preferences in managing ESG investments.</p></p>
