<p><p>The plastic welding equipment market comprises various types, including Electric Socket, Ultrasonic, Hot Plate, Spin, Hot Gas, Extrusion, Injection, High Frequency, and Laser welding. Electric Socket welding uses electrical resistance to join plastics. Ultrasonic employs high-frequency vibrations, while Hot Plate utilizes heated surfaces for fusion. Spin welding involves rotational motion for joining, and Hot Gas employs a jet of hot air. Extrusion and Injection welding involve molding processes, High Frequency uses electromagnetic energy, and Laser welding employs focused light for precision joining.</p></p>
