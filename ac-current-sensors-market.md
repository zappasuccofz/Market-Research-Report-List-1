<p><p>The AC current sensor market is divided into two main types: closed-loop and open-loop current sensors. Closed-loop current sensors measure current through feedback mechanisms, providing high accuracy and minimal distortion, making them ideal for precision applications. Open-loop current sensors, on the other hand, detect current without feedback, offering simpler designs and lower costs. While open-loop sensors are suitable for general applications, closed-loop sensors excel in environments requiring rigorous performance and reliability. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
