<p><ul><li>Low-level (LLW)</li><li>Intermediate-level (ILW)</li><li>High-level (HLW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radioactive Medical Waste market is categorized into three main types based on the level of radioactivity: Low-level waste (LLW), which includes materials like contaminated clothing and equipment with low radiation levels; Intermediate-level waste (ILW), comprising radioisotope medical materials requiring shielding during handling; and High-level waste (HLW), consisting of highly radioactive materials such as spent nuclear fuel and certain waste from medical procedures, necessitating robust containment and long-term storage solutions. Each type requires specific management and disposal strategies to ensure safety.</p></p>
