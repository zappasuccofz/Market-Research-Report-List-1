Coating processes involve applying an enteric layer to the capsules to protect sensitive ingredients from stomach acid, allowing for targeted release in the intestines. Non-coating processes focus on formulations that inherently provide enteric functionalities without the need for additional coatings. Both methods cater to specific therapeutic needs, enhancing drug delivery and improving bioavailability in various pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
