<p><p>The medical electronic nose market comprises various technologies for detecting and analyzing odors related to health conditions. MOS (Metal Oxide Sensors) are widely used for their sensitivity and response time. CP (Conductometric Polymer) sensors offer flexibility and can detect volatile compounds. QCM (Quartz Crystal Microbalance) sensors measure mass changes, enabling precise detection of substances. SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) sensors utilize sound waves to identify chemical compositions. Additionally, "Others" includes emerging technologies and hybrid sensors that enhance odor detection capabilities in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
