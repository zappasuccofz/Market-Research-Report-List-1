<p><ul><li>Single Zone</li><li>Multi Zone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ductless solutions, including mini-split systems, are categorized into single-zone and multi-zone markets. Single-zone systems are designed to condition a single area or room, offering targeted heating and cooling, making them suitable for smaller spaces or specific zones within a home. In contrast, multi-zone systems can service multiple rooms or areas simultaneously, allowing for customized climate control across larger spaces or entire homes. Both options provide energy efficiency and flexibility, enhancing indoor comfort without the need for ductwork.</p></p>
