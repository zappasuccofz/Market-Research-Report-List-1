<p><p>The automotive passive suspension systems market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle stability and comfort. MacPherson Suspension is a popular choice for its simplicity and space efficiency, commonly found in compact vehicles. Semi-independent Suspension combines independent and dependent features, providing a balance between comfort and handling, making it suitable for a range of vehicle types.</p></p>
