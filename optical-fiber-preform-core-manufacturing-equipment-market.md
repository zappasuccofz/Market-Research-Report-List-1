<p><ul><li>Core Deposition System</li><li>Core Sintering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber preform core manufacturing equipment market includes two primary types: Core Deposition Systems and Core Sintering Systems. Core Deposition Systems are utilized to precisely deposit layers of glass material to create the fiber's core, ensuring optimal refractive index profiles. Core Sintering Systems facilitate the consolidation of these deposited layers through high-temperature processes, enhancing strength and optical properties. Together, these systems are essential for producing high-quality optical fibers used in telecommunications and data transmission applications.</p></p>
