<p><p>The penetrating oil market is divided into two primary types: industrial-grade and food-grade. Industrial-grade penetrating oils are formulated for heavy-duty applications, providing lubrication and rust prevention in machinery, automotive, and manufacturing sectors. These oils often contain additives to enhance performance under extreme conditions.
