Infra-red detection systems utilize thermal imaging to identify temperature variations, signaling potential leaks. Fiber optic sensors provide real-time monitoring using light transmission, detecting changes in temperature and pressure along the pipeline. Together, these technologies enhance safety and environmental protection by enabling rapid leak identification and response in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
