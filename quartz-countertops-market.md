<p><strong>The Quartz Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Countertops market features primarily two production methods: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing a mixture of quartz crystals and resins under high pressure, resulting in a dense and durable surface. This method offers high efficiency for mass production. In contrast, casting molding pours the resin-quartz blend into molds, allowing for intricate designs and variations in thickness. This technique caters to custom shapes but may have longer curing times. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences in aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
