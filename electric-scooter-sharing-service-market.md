<p><p>Electric scooter sharing services are primarily categorized into two market types: dockless and station-based. Dockless systems allow users to locate and unlock scooters through a smartphone app without needing a designated docking station. These scooters can be parked anywhere within a designated operating area, providing flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based systems require users to pick up and drop off scooters at designated docking stations. This structure facilitates organized parking and can simplify fleet management for service providers.</p></p>
