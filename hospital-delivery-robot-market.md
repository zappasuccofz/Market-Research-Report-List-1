<p><p>The hospital delivery robot market features two primary navigation types: magnet navigation and laser navigation. Magnet navigation utilizes magnetic fields to guide robots along predetermined paths, ensuring precise movement in complex hospital environments. In contrast, laser navigation employs sensors and cameras to create real-time maps and navigate autonomously, allowing for more flexibility and adaptability in dynamic settings. Both technologies enhance operational efficiency, streamline deliveries of medical supplies, and improve overall workflow within healthcare facilities, ultimately contributing to better patient care.</p></p>
