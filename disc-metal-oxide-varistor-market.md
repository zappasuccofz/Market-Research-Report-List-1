<p><p>The Disc Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market is segmented into two main types: Low Voltage (LV) MOV and High-Medium Voltage (HV-MV) MOV. LV MOVs are designed for applications requiring protection at lower voltage levels, typically below 1000 volts, and are commonly used in consumer electronics and industrial equipment. In contrast, HV-MV MOVs cater to high and medium voltage applications, often exceeding 1000 volts, and are essential in power transmission, distribution systems, and industrial machinery. Both types serve critical roles in surge protection and voltage regulation.</p></p>
