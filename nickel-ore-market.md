<p><strong>The Nickel Ore Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nickeliferous Limonite</li><li>Garnierite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel ore market primarily consists of two significant types: nickeliferous limonite and garnierite. Nickeliferous limonite, typically found in lateritic deposits, is rich in nickel and used in the production of ferronickel, which is a vital input for stainless steel manufacturing. Garnierite, a silicate mineral, contains nickel and is mainly processed for nickel sulfate, which is essential for battery production, particularly in electric vehicles. Both types significantly contribute to meeting the growing global demand for nickel.</p></p>
