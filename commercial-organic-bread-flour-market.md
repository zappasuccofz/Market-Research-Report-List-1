<p><p>The Commercial Organic Bread Flour market features two primary types: Machine Mill Flour and Stone Mill Flour. Machine Mill Flour is produced using modern milling techniques that efficiently grind grains into fine flour while preserving essential nutrients, offering consistency and scalability. In contrast, Stone Mill Flour is made using traditional stone-ground methods, resulting in a coarser texture and richer flavor, often preferred for artisanal bread. Both types cater to the growing demand for organic and high-quality baking ingredients in the food industry.</p></p>
