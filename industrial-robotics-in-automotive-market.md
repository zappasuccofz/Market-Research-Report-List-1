<p><p>Industrial robotics in the automotive sector encompasses various robot types tailored for specific tasks. Articulated robots feature flexible joints for complex movements, ideal for assembly and welding. Cartesian robots operate on a three-axis system, performing precise tasks like material handling. SCARA robots excel in tasks requiring high speed and precision, such as assembly. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear movement for applications like packaging. Parallel robots offer high precision and load capacity, commonly used in assembly lines. Other variants further enhance automation efficiency.</p></p>
