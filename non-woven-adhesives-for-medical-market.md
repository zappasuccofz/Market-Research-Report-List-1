<p><p>Non-woven adhesives used in the medical market include Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBCs), Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefins (APAO), and Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA). SBCs offer high flexibility and strong adhesion, suitable for medical tapes and dressings. APAOs provide excellent thermal stability and low-temperature performance, ideal for sensitive applications. EVAs are known for their elasticity and compatibility, making them effective for skin contact applications. These adhesives are crucial for ensuring reliable bonding in various medical devices and products.</p></p>
