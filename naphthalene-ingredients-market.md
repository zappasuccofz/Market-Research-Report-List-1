<p><ul><li>Light Naphthalene Ingredients</li><li>Heavy Naphthalene Ingredients</li><li>Pure Naphthalene Ingredients</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Naphthalene Ingredients market is segmented into three main types: light naphthalene ingredients, heavy naphthalene ingredients, and pure naphthalene ingredients. Light naphthalene ingredients, typically derived from distillation, have lower molecular weight and are used in applications like solvents and intermediates. Heavy naphthalene ingredients, with higher molecular weight, serve as feedstocks for various chemical processes. Pure naphthalene ingredients, featuring high purity levels, are primarily used in the production of phthalic anhydride and in the manufacturing of mothballs and deodorizers.</p></p>
