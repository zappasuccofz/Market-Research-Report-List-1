<p><p>The Industrial IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) Power Semiconductors market comprises two primary types: discrete IGBTs and IGBT modules. Discrete IGBTs are individual semiconductor devices typically used in low to medium power applications, offering flexibility and easy integration into circuits. In contrast, IGBT modules contain multiple IGBT chips along with additional components like diodes and drivers, providing enhanced power handling capabilities, improved thermal management, and compact design for high-power applications, such as in inverters and motor drives.</p></p>
