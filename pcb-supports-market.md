<p><ul><li>With Adhesive Base</li><li>Without Adhesive Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB supports market is segmented into two main types: those with adhesive bases and those without. Adhesive base supports provide strong, permanent attachment to printed circuit boards, ensuring stability and reducing vibration. They are ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, supports without adhesive bases rely on mechanical fastening and offer flexibility for repositioning and easy removal. This variability allows manufacturers to choose the right type based on specific application needs, assembly processes, and product design considerations.</p></p>
