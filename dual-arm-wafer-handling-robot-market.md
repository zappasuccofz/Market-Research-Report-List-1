<p><p>The Dual Arm Wafer Handling Robot market comprises two primary types: atmospheric robots and vacuum robots. Atmospheric robots operate in clean environments, utilizing mechanical grips to handle wafers without compromising their integrity. In contrast, vacuum robots utilize suction mechanisms to securely lift and transfer wafers, minimizing risk of contamination and damage. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and precision in wafer handling processes, which are critical for producing high-quality electronic components.</p></p>
