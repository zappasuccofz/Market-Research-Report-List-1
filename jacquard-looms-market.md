<p><p>The Jacquard looms market is divided into two main types: mechanical and electronic Jacquard looms. Mechanical Jacquard looms utilize a series of controlled hooks and cards to create intricate patterns in woven fabrics. They are known for their simplicity and reliability in traditional textile manufacturing. In contrast, electronic Jacquard looms employ advanced digital technology, allowing for greater flexibility, design complexity, and automation. This enhances production efficiency and enables quicker changes to patterns, appealing to modern textile industries.</p></p>
