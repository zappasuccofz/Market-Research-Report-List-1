<p><p>Nomex honeycomb core materials are categorized based on cell geometry: hexagonal, rectangular (overexpanded), and pentagonal. Hexagonal cells, popular for their lightweight and high strength-to-weight ratio, provide excellent structural support in aerospace and automotive applications. Rectangular (overexpanded) cells offer versatility and a larger surface area for enhanced insulation and shock absorption, making them suitable for various industrial uses. Pentagonal cells combine the benefits of both previous types, facilitating unique structural properties while maintaining lightweight characteristics for specialized applications.</p></p>
