<p><p>Terahertz sensing technology encompasses two primary market types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing utilizes light waves in the terahertz frequency range to detect chemical, biological, and physical changes in materials, offering high resolution and precision. In contrast, electronic sensing relies on electronic components to measure terahertz signals, often focusing on speed and efficiency for applications in telecommunications and imaging. Both types play essential roles in diverse fields such as security, healthcare, and material characterization.</p></p>
