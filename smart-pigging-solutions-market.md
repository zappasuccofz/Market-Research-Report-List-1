<p><p>Smart pigging solutions encompass various market types, primarily Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL), Ultrasonic Testing (UT), and others. MFL utilizes magnetic fields to detect anomalies and corrosion in pipelines, making it efficient for volumetric assessment. Ultrasonic Testing employs sound waves to measure wall thickness and identify flaws, offering high accuracy and effectiveness for various materials. Additionally, other methods may include laser profiling and guided wave testing, each serving specialized applications in pipeline integrity management and inspection, enhancing operational safety.</p></p>
