<p><p>The Regenerative Artificial Skin market comprises three primary types: Temporary Skin Material, Permanent Skin Material, and Composite Skin Material. Temporary Skin Material is designed for short-term use, providing protection and facilitating healing in acute wounds before natural skin regeneration occurs. Permanent Skin Material offers long-lasting solutions for severe burns and chronic wounds, integrating with the body over time. Composite Skin Material combines both temporary and permanent elements, offering immediate protection while promoting natural healing, thereby enhancing overall therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
