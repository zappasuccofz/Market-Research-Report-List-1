<p><p>The polycarbonate lenses market features various types tailored to different vision needs. Single vision lenses provide a single corrective prescription for distance or reading. Bifocal lenses have two distinct sections for near and far vision, while trifocal lenses add a middle section for intermediate distances. Progressive lenses offer a seamless transition between multiple vision zones without visible lines, catering to presbyopic individuals. Each type is valued for its lightweight, impact-resistant properties, making polycarbonate lenses a popular choice in eyewear.</p></p>
