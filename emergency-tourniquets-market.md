<p><strong>The Emergency Tourniquets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-pneumatic</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency tourniquet market is primarily divided into two types: non-pneumatic and pneumatic tourniquets. Non-pneumatic tourniquets, often used in emergency scenarios, rely on mechanical tension to compress blood vessels and control bleeding. They are typically made from rigid materials and are easy to apply in urgent situations. Pneumatic tourniquets, on the other hand, utilize air pressure within an inflatable cuff to achieve compression, allowing for precise control over blood flow. Both types play vital roles in trauma and surgical care.</p></p>
