<p><strong>The Tantalum Target Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tantalum target market is primarily divided into two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces used in physical vapor deposition processes, providing uniform material deposition for various applications like semiconductors. In contrast, rotating targets are designed to enhance deposition efficiency by continuously introducing fresh material during the process, reducing the risk of target overheating and uneven wear. Both types are essential in industries such as electronics and coatings, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
