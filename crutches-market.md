<p><p>The crutches market primarily consists of three types: underarm crutches, forearm crutches, and tetrapod crutches. Underarm crutches provide support by resting under the arms and are commonly used for temporary mobility issues. Forearm crutches offer enhanced mobility and independence, as they use cuffs around the forearms for support, making them suitable for longer-term use. Tetrapod crutches feature a four-legged base for improved stability, ideal for users needing additional support and balance while walking. This diverse range addresses various mobility needs.</p></p>
