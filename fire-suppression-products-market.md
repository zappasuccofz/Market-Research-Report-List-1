<p><p>The fire suppression products market includes several key types. Dry chemical fire suppression systems use powdered agents to extinguish flames quickly, effective for various fire classes. Foam fire suppression creates a blanket over flammable liquids to prevent re-ignition, suitable for industrial and commercial settings. CO2 fire suppression displaces oxygen, suffocating the fire, ideal for enclosed spaces with sensitive equipment. The "Others" category encompasses alternative methods like water mist and inert gas systems, catering to diverse fire protection needs.</p></p>
