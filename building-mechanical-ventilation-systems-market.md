<p><p>The building mechanical ventilation systems market encompasses various types, including exhaust ventilation, supply ventilation, balanced ventilation, and energy recovery ventilation. Exhaust ventilation systems effectively remove stale air, creating a negative pressure that draws in fresh air. Supply ventilation introduces fresh air, maintaining positive pressure. Balanced ventilation combines both approaches, ensuring equal air exchange. Energy recovery ventilation enhances efficiency by transferring heat between outgoing and incoming air, optimizing energy use. Each type caters to different building needs, enhancing indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
