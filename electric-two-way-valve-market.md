<p><strong>The Electric Two Way Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting Electric Two Way Valve</li><li>Pilot Operated Electric Two Way Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Two Way Valve market comprises two primary types: Direct Acting Electric Two Way Valves and Pilot Operated Electric Two Way Valves. Direct Acting valves operate directly with an electric actuator, providing quick response and precise control for flow regulation. In contrast, Pilot Operated valves utilize a smaller pilot valve to control a larger main valve, allowing for efficient operation under high pressure and larger flow capacities. Both types are essential for automated control in various industrial applications.</p></p>
