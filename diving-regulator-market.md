<p><ul><li>First Stage Diving Regulator</li><li>Second Stage Diving Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving regulator market consists of two main types: first stage and second stage regulators. The first stage regulator connects to the tank and reduces high-pressure air to a manageable level, delivering it to the second stage. The second stage regulator is the mouthpiece that further reduces the pressure, allowing divers to breathe comfortably underwater. Together, these components ensure a safe and efficient breathing system for divers, catering to varying levels of experience and environmental conditions in the diving community.</p></p>
