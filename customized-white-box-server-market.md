<p><p>The customized white box server market consists of three main types: rack-mount servers, blade servers, and whole cabinet servers. Rack-mount servers are designed to fit into standardized racks, offering flexibility and efficient space utilization. Blade servers house multiple server blades within a single chassis, optimizing power and cooling while minimizing footprint. Whole cabinet servers encompass complete systems in standalone enclosures, providing comprehensive solutions for large-scale deployments. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, scalability, and manageability.</p></p>
