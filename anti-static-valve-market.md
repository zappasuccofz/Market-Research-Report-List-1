<p><p>The anti-static valve market includes various types, each serving specific applications in fluid control. Globe valves, with their spherical body, provide precise flow regulation. Gate valves offer minimal flow resistance when fully open. Ball valves ensure quick shut-off with a rotating ball mechanism. Butterfly valves are space-efficient for large volumes, while diaphragm valves utilize a flexible membrane for durability and low maintenance. Plug valves allow for easy flow control with a rotating plug, and needle valves offer fine adjustment of flow rates.</p></p>
