<p><strong>The Organic Rice Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polished glutinous rice(sticky rice)</li><li>Indica(long-shaped rice)</li><li>Polished round-grained rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic rice market encompasses various types, each catering to different culinary needs. Polished glutinous rice, often referred to as sticky rice, is prized for its adhesive texture, making it ideal for desserts and traditional dishes. Indica rice, characterized by its long grains, is commonly used in a variety of savory meals due to its fluffy texture when cooked. Polished round-grained rice, known for its short and plump grains, is favored in dishes requiring creaminess, like risottos and sushi. Each type serves unique market demands driven by consumer preferences and culinary traditions.</p></p>
